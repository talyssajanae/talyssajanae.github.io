Introduction

Slocal Business

Slocal Business is a digital magazine that will highlight local, family-owned companies in San Luis Obispo County to amplify the owners' voices of their craft, while keeping in mind ethical and sustainable practices. We want to give exposure to those who might be looking for a place to eat, shop, or explore San Luis Obispo and inspire people to support local businesses and shop-owners.
